Gastown Steam Clock

The Steam Clock was built in 1977 by Raymond Saunders and Doug Smith. The clock was built as a monumnet and placed over a steam grate. Every quarter hour, the Steam Clock whistles and shoots steam from its five whistles.

My next door neighbors!

I live in the red building right next to the Fire House in Gastown. You can hear the sirens all day long. The firefighters are very nice and always say good morning when I go to College.

Fire Hall No 1!

This is the first Fire Hall in Vancouver and possibly the first Fire Hall in North America. It is no longer used as a Fire Hall, it was converted into the Fire Hall Arts Center in the 1970s.
